Data Sensitivity Classification Policy

Examples:

# Secret:
- Strategic business plans
- Unrelease financial or market-sensitive details
- Details of unanounced financial mergers, products or services

  # Confidential
  - Personal customer information
  - Project or system documentation that could damage the business
  - Related to PCI DSS or similar
 
  # Internal
  - Instruction packs
  - Intranet pages, newsletters, group & team communications
  - Out of office messages
 
    # Public
    - Anything that is approved for release to the public
    - Already available from public records
